The DBA who makes the numbers move

I administer Microsoft SQL Server across high-availability, security-sensitive environments — six instances today, two of them failover-clustered with Always On. Day to day that means owning backup and restore, replication, capacity planning, patching, and version migrations through SQL Server 2019, plus the SSIS, SSRS, and SSAS stack.

What I care about most is measurable impact: queries that run faster, systems that stay up, and data that stays protected. I work with confidential government data, document and present my work for technical and business audiences, and I'm currently leading database modernization to the cloud with security and cost as first priorities.
